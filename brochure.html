The villa

Built for slow mornings
and long tables.

Crystal Springs sits off the Varai road outside Karjat, where the Sahyadri range flattens into farmland and the air stays several degrees cooler than the city. The villa is entirely yours for the length of your stay — no shared corridors, no other bookings on the property.

Glass-walled rooms open onto a wraparound patio; a hammock catches the morning light outside one bedroom, a private balcony outside another. Downstairs, the pool's small waterfall runs quietly enough to hear the birds over it.

Everything a group actually uses is here: a private pool and jacuzzi, a full game zone, open-air dining, gardens, and a kitchen team that handles every meal so you don't have to.
