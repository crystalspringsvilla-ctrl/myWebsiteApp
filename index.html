The villa

Built for slow mornings and long tables.

Crystal Springs sits off the Varai road outside Karjat, where the Sahyadri range flattens into farmland and the air stays several degrees cooler than the city. The villa is entirely yours for the length of your stay — no shared corridors, no other bookings on the property.

Glass-walled rooms open onto a wraparound patio; a hammock catches the morning light outside one bedroom, a private balcony outside another. Downstairs, the pool's small waterfall runs quietly enough to hear the birds over it.

Before you book

Cancellation & refund policy.

We book the entire villa exclusively for one group at a time, so cancellations affect us more than a single-room hotel booking would. Here's exactly how refunds work.

1. Booking cancellations & refund slabs

Refunds for villa booking charges (base villa rate and extra guest charges) are based on how far ahead of check-in (1:00 PM) you cancel:

  • 15+ days before check-in100% refund
  • 7–14 days before check-in50% refund
  • Less than 7 days before check-inNo refund

2. Food & add-on services (BBQ)

Ingredients and kitchen staff are arranged in advance for your custom menu, so:

  • Cancellations of food or BBQ made within 48 hours of check-in are non-refundable.
  • Cancellations made more than 48 hours before check-in get a 100% refund.

3. Refund processing timeline

Once our team approves a refund, it's initiated right away and credited back to the original payment method used (card, net banking, UPI, or wallet).

Refunds typically take 5–7 working days to reflect in your account.

4. No-shows & early departures

  • No-shows (arriving without prior cancellation) are treated as a full stay — no refund.
  • Early departures aren't refunded or pro-rated for the unused nights.

5. Force majeure & severe weather

If extreme weather, a natural disaster, a state-enforced lockdown, or another circumstance beyond our control makes hosting you unsafe, we reserve the right to cancel your booking. In that case, you'll get either a 100% refund, or the option to reschedule to an alternative date of equal value, subject to availability.
